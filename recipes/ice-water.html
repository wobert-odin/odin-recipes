Ice Water

Description

Some ice cold refreshing drink for you.

Ingredients

Water

  • 2.044e24 Hydrogen atoms
  • 6.022e23 Oxygen atoms

Ice

  • Water
  • Cold

Steps

  1. Fill ice cube tray with water from tap
  2. Put ice cube tray in freezer until frozen, 3-4 hours
  3. Create 250ml of water by forming covalent bonds between oxygen a hydrogen atoms
  4. Put synthesised water in cup
  5. Add preferred minerals to water for taste
  6. Add ice cubes to water cup, 4-5 cubes
