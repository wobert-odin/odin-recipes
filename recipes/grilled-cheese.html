Grilled Cheese

Description

Cheese and bread. No better combo.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Cheese slice

Steps

  1. Heat pan
  2. Take two slices of bread and butter one side of each
  3. Place bread on pan, butter side down
  4. Place cheese on bread
  5. Place other slice of bread on top, butter side up
  6. Cook until bottom is browned, then flip
  7. Continue cooking until cheese is melted
